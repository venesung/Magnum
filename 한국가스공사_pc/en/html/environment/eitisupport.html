EITI Support Statement

The EITI is a coalition of governments, companies and civil societies which sets a global standard for managing revenues from natural resources. The objective is to strengthen governance through increased transparency on the revenues governments receive from the extractive industries. It is implemented by countries with such industries and supported by other countries and stakeholders.

Countries rich in natural resources such as oil, gas, and mining tend to under-perform economically, have a higher incidence of conflict, and suffer from poor governance. These effects are not inevitable and it is hoped that by encouraging greater transparency in countries rich in these resources, some of the potential negative impacts can be mitigated. Implementation of policies that ensure transparency and accountability will help secure more stable investment environment and create lasting benefits for related parties.
