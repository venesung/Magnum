Business System

As a public enterprise, KOGAS is in charge of wholesale of natural gas in the domestic market while 30 private city gas companies are granted with exclusive retail sales rights within their respective regions. KOGAS imports LNG and distributes it to consumers across the nation. Seventeen power generation plants of ten power generation companies supply the gas to their end users within their respective regions.
