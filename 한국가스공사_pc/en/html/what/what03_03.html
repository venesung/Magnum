LNG Projects

  • GLNG Project, Australia
    • project : to convert coal seam natural gas (CSG) to liquefied natural gas (LNG) for export to global markets.

      - CSG : Coal Seam Gas (Coal-bed methane, CBM)

      GLNG Project
      Upstream development of gas fields in the Bowen and Surat Basins in south-western Queensland
      Midstream construction of a 420 kilometre underground gas transmission pipeline to Gladstone
      Downstream construction of a two-train LNG processing facility on Curtis Island, off the coastline of Gladstone
    • Ownership Structure : Santos 30%; PETRONAS 27.5%; Total 27.5%; and KOGAS 15%.
  • Prelude FLNG Project, Australia
    • Location: Browse Basin, Australia
    • Depth : ~250 metres
    • Category : Floating liquefied natural gas
    • Interest : Shell 67.5%, Inpex 17.5%, CPC 5%, Kogas 10%
    • Fields : Prelude and potentially other Shell natural gas assets in the region
    • FLNG facility production capacity : At least 5.3 million tonnes per annum (mtpa) of liquids - 3.6 mtpa of LNG, 1.3 mtpa of condensate and 0.4 mtpa of LPG
    • Key contractors : Technip Samsung Consortium
