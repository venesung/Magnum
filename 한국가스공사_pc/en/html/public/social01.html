Activities

As a responsible corporate citizen, KOGAS launched Cheongyeon Service Corps in 1999, dedicated to organized social service activities. In 2007, we set up an independent department dedicated to strategic practices in social contribution activities.

ORGANIZATION & DECISION - MAKING SYSTEM

KOGAS maintains organized social contribution activities through a dedicated department. All proposals are delivered to the related department for objective review and then referred to a resolutions council.

  1. Resolution by President / Management Committee
  2. Deliberation By social Contribution Project committee
  3. Review by Social Contribution Department
  4. Reception of External Proposal
