Striving to Build a Happier Society

Under our corporate philosophy of "better energy, better world," KOGAS practices sharing and caring in order to build a happier world for everyone. In addition to making contributions toward the national economic growth and public convenience, the Corporation, as a public enterprise, fulfills its corporate social responsibilities by returning profits to society. By conserving the environment and our cultural assets, the Corporation delivers dreams and hopes to our communities and underprivileged neighbors, and builds a happier world with blue energy.

DISCOUNT RATE FOR CHARITIES

As part of our initiatives to stabilize the livelihood of marginalized people, KOGAS offers product discounts to social charities. Having commenced in July 2001, the number of beneficiaries reached 9,836 charities for the disabled, elderly and children protection facilities across the nation. By applying the lowest industrial gas rate,
we discounted a total of KRW 23 million in 2007.
