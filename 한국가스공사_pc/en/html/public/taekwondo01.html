Introduction

KOGAS TaeKwondo Team

Founded in August 1997, the KOGAS TAEKWONDO team announced in January 2003 a new beginning under the new leadership of Mr. Jongman Park (Manager) and Mr. Jaeyun Shin (Coach). We have come a long way to become a globally renowned professional TAEKWONDO team.

The KOGAS TAEKWONDO team has won several gold medals in many prestigious competitions including the '98 Bangkok Asian Games, '01, '03, and '05 World TAEKWONDO Championships.

The KOGAS TAEKWONDO team relentlessly strives to uphold the passion, pride, and dignity of this ancient national sport of Korea and we therefore seek your continued support.
